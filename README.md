
# MindMate AI – Exam Wellness Copilot

## Overview

MindMate AI is an AI-powered mental wellness platform designed for students preparing for competitive exams such as JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE, and CUET.

The platform helps students monitor their emotional well-being, identify hidden stress triggers, track mood patterns, and receive personalized AI-powered support throughout their exam journey.

## Problem Statement

Students preparing for high-stakes examinations often face:

- Stress and anxiety
- Burnout
- Lack of motivation
- Self-doubt
- Emotional exhaustion

Traditional mood trackers only record emotions but fail to identify the root causes behind stress.

## Solution

MindMate AI uses Generative AI to:

- Analyze daily journal entries
- Detect hidden emotional patterns
- Identify stress triggers
- Predict burnout risk
- Provide personalized wellness recommendations
- Offer motivational support through an AI companion

## Key Features

### Daily Wellness Journal
Students can log:

- Study progress
- Emotions
- Challenges
- Confidence levels

### AI Emotion Analysis
Automatically analyzes:

- Mood Score
- Stress Level
- Confidence Score
- Burnout Risk

### Hidden Stress Trigger Detection
Identifies recurring stress sources such as:

- Difficult subjects
- Exam pressure
- Poor mock test performance
- Time management issues

### AI Wellness Recommendations
Provides personalized suggestions to improve:

- Productivity
- Mental wellness
- Study-life balance

### Future Me Motivation
Generates encouraging messages from a student's future successful self.

### Analytics Dashboard
Interactive visualizations for:

- Mood trends
- Stress trends
- Confidence trends
- Wellness score tracking
