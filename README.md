## Why MindMate AI?

Competitive exam preparation is not only an academic challenge but also a mental and emotional one. Students often struggle with stress, burnout, anxiety, and self-doubt without access to timely support.

MindMate AI acts as an intelligent wellness companion that helps students understand their emotional patterns, identify hidden stress triggers, and receive personalized guidance throughout their preparation journey.
